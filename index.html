Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about your HVAC or electrical systems? Find answers to some common inquiries below. If you don't see your question, feel free to contact us directly!

What is the recommended frequency for HVAC maintenance?

We recommend having your HVAC system professionally serviced at least once a year, ideally in the spring for your AC and in the fall for your heating system. This ensures optimal performance, extends lifespan, and helps prevent unexpected breakdowns.

How can I improve my home's indoor air quality?

Improving indoor air quality involves several steps: regularly changing air filters, professional duct cleaning, using air purifiers, ensuring proper ventilation, and maintaining appropriate humidity levels. Gabriel's Air Care can assess your home and recommend tailored solutions.

When should I consider replacing my electrical panel?

Consider a panel upgrade if your home is old (20+ years), you frequently experience tripped breakers, your lights flicker, you're adding major new appliances, or your current panel is outdated (e.g., fuse box). Our electricians can perform a safety inspection and recommend if an upgrade is necessary.

Do you offer emergency HVAC or electrical services?

Yes, we understand that HVAC and electrical emergencies can happen at any time. While we primarily operate within our business hours, we strive to provide responsive assistance for urgent issues. Please call us directly for immediate support.
